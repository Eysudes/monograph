  • 1st Year: Foundation in Mathematics

    Master foundational mathematical concepts including Mathematics in the Modern World, Calculus I & II, Fundamental Concepts of Mathematics, and Linear Algebra. Build strong problem-solving abilities and develop a rigorous understanding of core mathematical principles.

    View 1st Year Resources

  • 2nd Year: Advanced Mathematics & Physics

    Expand your knowledge with Probability, Calculus III, General Physics I & II, Fundamentals of Computing I, Advanced Calculus I, and Differential Equations. Deepen mathematical understanding while exploring physics applications and introduction to computational thinking.

    View 2nd Year Resources

  • 3rd Year: Specialization & Applications

    Explore specialized topics including Abstract Algebra II, Database Management, Advanced Calculus II, Java Programming, Modern Geometry, Elementary Number Theory, Web Development, and Python Programming. Combine pure mathematics with practical computer science applications.

    View 3rd Year Resources

  • 4th Year: Advanced Theory & Analysis

    Master advanced mathematical theory with Statistical Theory, Complex Analysis, Real Analysis, Numerical Analysis, and Operations Research I. Prepare for graduate studies or professional careers through rigorous analysis and mathematical modeling coursework.

    View 4th Year Resources
